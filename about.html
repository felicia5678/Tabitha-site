TABITHA HOME

Tabitha home is a home for vulnerable children.

Its purpose is making kids smile again.
It was established in the year 2014.
It was founded by Mrs. Febisola Okonkwo.It is located in Joyce 'B' road.
Tabitha home has over 50 children.
It takes care of the children and provides their basic needs e.g transport & shelter etc
Tabitha home is in collaboration with Help Initiative.

Tabitha Home takes care of the children's needs such as:

  • Shelter

  • Transportation

  • Medical services

  • Moral support

  • Clothing

  • Education

  • Spiritual needs

  • Food

  • Entertainment

  • Motivations
